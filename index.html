Their Stories

Miracle messages started with a question. Why, in a nation with more homes than homeless  do so many people suffer needlessly against the elements, often dying in the cold, from sickness, or even starvation whilst the impassioned populace steps over them negating their struggle, and thus their humanity completely? While the bulk of this dehumanizing nature can be surmised as an overreaching effect of the nature of capitalism itself such a simple analysis simply would not do when the question of homelessness is not resolved, nor even properly addressed. We at Miracle Messages believe that every life is intrinsically valuable, and that every person has a place. Do we not all deserve a place to call home? We exist to help you find those that you have lost, but also to help those that are lost find their way to you. Help us on our Journey.

Family And Anonymous Postings

While we prefer you sign up we recognize the importance of being able to submit information anonymously. Through our families tab you have the ability not only to browse cases that have been opened in hopes of finding your missing person, but you also have the ability to submit data on persons you have met that are in need of assistance. Your information could save a life. Please do not hesitate to post.

Volunteers

Volunteers are members who have signed up to Miracle Messages. As a volunteer you have to ability to add and browse cases at your discrection. You can also submit contact info so we can reach you if anyone happens to find your person. Volunteers have the power to take an active role in changing the lives of those who are suffering at the behest of an oppressive and destructive system.
